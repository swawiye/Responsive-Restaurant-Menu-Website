Our Genesis Story

Soleil means 'sun' in French. The sun is the main source of energy for life here on the earth. The sun supports life by giving energy to nourish it. Our restaurant was born as a place where you can not only indulge in energising meals, but also recharge yourself by enjoying the vibes and socialize. We provide an ambient surrounding filled with warm sunlight, high spirits and calming music. When you are at Soleil, times stops to enable you to fully immerse yourself in the present and recharge yourself with sun-like energy. We invite you to go through this calming and energising experience at Soleil today. Feel the energy of the sun
